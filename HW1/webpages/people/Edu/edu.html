Hacking

The image above is from when I participated in the European Cyber Security, a European hacking competition. With the Dutch national team. I've been hacking for around 4 years now. My aunt introduced me to a hacking organisation a few years ago called "Challenge the Cyber". They host a lot of in-person and online capture-the-flag events. These are events where challenges are created, which you can hack to gain "flags", earning you points. The person or team with the most points at the end of the event is the winner.
